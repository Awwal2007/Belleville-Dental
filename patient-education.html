Dental Health Tips

How often should I visit the dentist?

It’s recommended to visit your dentist every 6 months for a check-up and cleaning. Regular visits allow your dentist to catch problems early, prevent gum disease, and maintain healthy teeth. In some cases, like if you have braces or gum issues, your dentist may suggest more frequent visits.
